App overview

Lion AR is available for Android users. It works on an Android phone with 70 MB of free storage and Samsung Galaxy S7 or better required for navigation features. We developed in using the Unity Game Engine. We utilized Vuforia and ARCore, as our Augmented Reality frameworks. The app shows information about locations of interest that are displayed with augmented reality at key places. Visitors can use in-app navigation to find buildings on campus to explore. It is softly-guided – users can go where they please at any time or let the system guide them. A saving feature allows them to replay any interesting information later without the need for an anchor. The app is designed to be modular and easy to extend – adding new locations requires only a few new changes. Our app can quickly be repurposed to handle large events or provide tours on other campuses.
