Lion AR

Lets Place A Virtual World
On top of the Real World!

Thank you for stopping by our Capstone Project website. Our project is an Augmented Reality Android mobile application, ‘Lion AR’. The application assists incoming students, current students, or visitors by providing real-time navigation building-to-building and room-to-room. Currently, we are focusing on the locations/rooms at Educational Activities Building (EAB). On the tour, inside each room and location, there are QR-like markers placed in meaningful locations. When the user's camera detects a marker, a virtual ‘signpost’ will come to life and give them more information about that location. These signposts currently include voice-overs and text. When the user is ready to go to the next room on their tour, they will be directed by an interactive map of the campus.
